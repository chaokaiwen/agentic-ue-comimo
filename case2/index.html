Agentic UE-CoMIMO
Wearable Blind-Spot Sensing Radar · SINR Edition

A scooter creator is live-streaming when a cyclist approaches from the rear blind spot. The phone probes; the smartwatch and scooter CPE receive the bistatic echo. Each link's instantaneous SINR (radar equation, self-interference / direct-path residuals, ADC quantization) is mapped through the paper's waveform-CFAR Pd(SINR) lookup.

Three policies, same physics: No-Coop senses with the watch only. Static-CPE is pre-configured cooperation: it delegates sensing to the higher-gain CPE from the start and idles the watch, so it beats No-Coop immediately, but it never re-plans. The Proposed agent recruits the CPE when watch confidence drops, fuses watch+CPE while both live, and falls back to phone–watch sensing on a CPE failure report. A body blockage blinds the watch (No-Coop fails); a CPE failure blinds the delegated Static-CPE; only the agent keeps the warning through both.
